"Welcome! I'm here to help you transform your teaching with modern technology. Join me for hands-on training sessions where you'll learn to build interactive, engaging classroom resources. No technical experience needed—just bring your creativity and ideas!"

**Duration: ~20 seconds**

"I offer tailored training sessions for schools and individual teachers. Whether you need a one-off workshop to get started, or ongoing support throughout the year, I'll guide you step-by-step. We'll work together to create the exact resources your students need—from interactive quizzes to custom learning games."

## Section 2: Training Programs
**Duration: ~25 seconds**

"My training is progressive and practical. We start with the foundations—understanding how AI can assist your teaching. Then we move to hands-on creation, where you'll build your first classroom resource. Finally, you'll master advanced techniques to create truly unique materials. Each session is interactive, supportive, and designed to fit your schedule and skill level."

**Duration: ~25 seconds**

"Imagine creating custom interactive quizzes that adapt to your students' needs. Or developing educational games that make learning fun and engaging. ## Section 5: Contact
**Duration: ~15 seconds**

## Recording Notes:
- **Tone**: Friendly, encouraging, professional but approachable
- **Pace**: Moderate—clear enough for understanding but energetic enough to maintain engagement
- **Personality**: Like a supportive colleague who's excited to help, not a salesperson
- **Emphasis**: Highlight practical benefits and ease of use
- **Pauses**: Natural pauses at commas and periods for clarity
- **Energy**: Start each section with slightly elevated energy, maintain warmth throughout
